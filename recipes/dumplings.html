Dumplings

Description

Dumplings are Chinese comfort food at its best. With limitless possibilities for variation and experimentation, itâ€™s a fun meal to prepare and eat with family and friends.

Ingredients

  • Wonton dough, defrosted
  • Pointed or white cabbage
  • Lettuce heads
  • Fresh ginger
  • Garlic clove
  • Beef mince
  • Caster sugar
  • Sesame oil
  • Fish sauce
  • Chilli flake
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Shred and finely chop the cabbage. Thinly slice the onion. Peel and finely grate the ginger and garlic. Put everything in a bowl and add the beef mince, sugar, sesame oil, fish sauce, chilli flakes, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Lay out a few wonton dough at a time and place a heaping teaspoon of filling in the middle. Moisten the edges with a little water. Pinch upwards to form a dumpling. Steam for 10-12 minutes.
  3. Whisk together the ingredients for the dipping sauce.
  4. Fry the broccoli in butter & rapeseed oil for 2-3 minutes. Add finely grated garlic and chili flakes. Fry for a few minutes more. Season to taste with salt.
  5. Serve dumplings
