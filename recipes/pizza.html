Pizza

Description

Pepperoni pizza is a bestseller at any pizzeria. Here's an easy recipe and, if you use store bought dough, it’s almost faster to make from scratch than picking one up from your local restaurant. If you have time, give our quick-and-easy homemade dough a try for a truly authentic pizza experience!

Ingredients

  • Store-bought pizza dough or homemade pizza dough
  • Puck® Shredded Creamy Mozzarella Cheese
  • Sliced beef pepperoni
  • Red onion, sliced
  • Dried oregano

Steps

  1. Place two baking trays in the oven to heat.
  2. Thinly roll out the dough into two round pizza bases (approximately 30 cm in diameter).
  3. Place the pizza bases on two pieces of baking paper and spread the tomato sauce on top. Add the grated cheese, pepperoni and onion and sprinkle with oregano.
  4. Pull the pizzas with the baking paper onto the hot baking trays and bake the pizzas for approximately 10 minutes at 275 °C. Serve immediately.
